Mathura Vrindavan 2 Days Tour - Special

2 Days Mathura - Vrindavan Tour - Special
Day
1

Mathura

Our chauffeur or driver will arrive at your preferred location or anywhere in Delhi at 6:00 AM to pick you up. We will then drive for approximately 2 hours to reach Mathura.

During the Vrindavan tours, we will visit the Sri Krishna Janamabhoomi and Dwarkadheesh Temple. In the afternoon, we will proceed to Gokul, where you can explore the Chinta Haran Temple, Brahmand Ghat, Gokul Temple, and Chaurasi Khamba.

After sightseeing in Gokul, we will continue your journey to the hotel to check in and freshen up. In the evening, enjoy the beautiful Evening Yamuna Aarti. You will also have the option to stay overnight.

Day
2

Vrindavan

After breakfast, we will proceed to Vrindavan. Upon arrival, we will visit the Mata Vaishno Devi Temple, ISKCON Temple, Bankey Bihari Temple, Nidhivan, Rangaji Temple, Pagalbaba Temple, Birla Temple, and Shanti Kunj.

In the evening, we will head to Prem Mandir to enjoy the captivating Light and Sound Show. After the sightseeing activities and the show, it will be time to drop you off at your preferred location in Delhi or anywhere else in Delhi.
